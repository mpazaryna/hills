About the Club

Located off Back Creek, Eastport in historic Annapolis and founded in 1929 The Eastport Democratic Club serves as the community's meeting and gathering place, for all things Eastport and Annapolis. The EDC is open to members and their guests daily from 5.00pm to 12.00am.
